Ramakrishna Vivekananda Ashrama, Yelahanka - Bengaluru

Welcome to Ramakrishna Vivekananda Ashrama, a spiritual center dedicated to the teachings of Sri Ramakrishna, Swami Vivekananda, and Holy Mother Sri Sarada Devi. Our ashrama serves as a beacon of spiritual light, offering various programs and activities aimed at the spiritual upliftment of all.

Located in the serene surroundings of Yelahanka, Bengaluru, our ashrama provides an ideal environment for spiritual practice, study, and service.

Our Mission

Following the ideals of Sri Ramakrishna and Swami Vivekananda, we are committed to:

  • Promoting the universal teachings of Vedanta
  • Fostering harmony among all religions
  • Serving humanity as a form of worship
  • Providing spiritual guidance to sincere seekers

Regular Activities

  • Daily worship and arati
  • Weekly discourses on Vedanta and spiritual texts
  • Meditation sessions
  • Celebration of religious festivals
  • Service activities for the local community

Visit us to experience peace, spiritual growth, and the joy of service.
